About Us

Turquoise offers essential but cosy

Turquoise is a friendly hostel hidden up an enigmatic flight of steps in Nablus' Old City, a dense place to explore; even on the same street is a variety of food and other souq shops, a bathhouse, mosque, and soap factory with free tours. The hostel occupies a beautiful old property with a bright, well furnished courtyard, kitchen and living space, and dorms, with exposed stonework, open stairs and a terrace with views over Nablus stunning both day and night. The welcome is warm and good-humoured.
